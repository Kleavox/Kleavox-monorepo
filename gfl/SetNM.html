 SPEQ-Sets These Dolls have no MOD, they are sorted in GZ SPEQ introduction order.
3
AA-12 When equipped with both SPEQs, AA-12's ICD is reduced to 4 seconds. After attacking a number of times, gain the following buffs for the rest of the battle:
3: +10 Armor
5: +100% DMG
10: 30% DMG Reduction		 AA-12 is a strange attempt at a DPS SG, dealing minor damage in an "AoE" around targets (that is so small it might as well not be AoE) and having increased RoF and Knockback chance during her Skill.

With her SPEQ-set, she remains a strange SG who now has a bit more Armor and... now can't be immune to damage because the ranking SPEQ is a Slug with no DMG Reduction effect. Multiplicative Damage Reduction is applied after Armor, so it's generally really bad for SGs.
Overall, this SPEQ-set is not very useful.
4
PKP When equipped with both SPEQs, PKP's Skill proccing also grants +5% Armor for allies on her tiles for 5s, stacking multiplicatively up to 6 times. PKP is one of the older MGs, and while almost every other similar MG has since faded into complete irrelevancy, PKP's login SPEQ has managed to at least let her DPS be somewhat reasonable, provided the conditions are met.

With her SPEQ-set, she attempts to overcome her issue of having no Armor tiles, and the result is... somewhat disappointing. With an Ammo Box, she will always lose all her stacks during the reload, meaning that incoming damage can fluctuate a LOT. With a Bipod, this is slightly mitigated but RNG is still RNG and you can still drop to 0 Armor stacks, while usually maintaining 2-3 during reload. As a result, relying on her for Armor buffs is just not a good idea, and she becomes a shining example of why doing nothing for 6 seconds repeatedly throughout a fight is just so bad.

While her DPS being somewhat reasonable, she still is an MG with all the issues that entails (random targeting, reloading), and she should only be considered if these issues do not matter.
5
SR-3MP When equipped with both SPEQs, SR-3MP gains Surehit during her Skill. Additionally, all allies have their Movement Speed increased to 15 for the rest of the battle. SR-3MP is an SMG with a rather significant DMG self-buff, who has always been considered inferior to grenadiers in an Offtank position because of the lack of initial burst damage and the problem of losing links in the frontline.

However, with the GZ SPEQ granting +25% Final Damage, she actually does pretty decent sustained DPS. Her new SPEQ-set makes Evasion a non-issue. She brute forces through almost all commonly seen amounts of Armor with the sheer amount of DMG buffs she gets. On top of this, she also provides the entire team with HG Movement Speed, which can be very useful in some situations.
All that being said, while her DPS is now up to par with decent AR and RF self-buffers, she still retains SMG targeting (random) as a big weakness.

If you need a backline DPS with Surehit and the targeting isn't an issue, SR-3MP is now a viable option to consider.
6
WA2000 When equipped with both SPEQs, WA2000 gains 1 AP and 1 Acc per attack per link, lasting 15s.
Against Armored enemies, she gains additional Damage equal to 50% of her AP.		 Traditionally, WA2000 has been the "baseline performance Generalist RF", and is used as a reference for whether any other newer RF is usable or not.

With her new SPEQ-Set, she is much, much stronger than before, vastly outperforming most other Generalist RFs. With the high RoF from her Skill, she easily gains dozens of stacks in seconds, boosting her AP and Acc to extremely high levels.
The new Ammo SPEQ alone is already far stronger than any other AP Ammo, acting as a free extra Cape with the Crit Damage buff, and combined with the Set effect, she does massive amounts of damage to enemies with Armor.
On the other hand, against evasive enemies, WA2000 hits far more consistently than other RFs at Night, because the Acc is a flat buff (and thus applied after the Night Battle Acc Penalty).

Overall, this SPEQ-Set returns WA2000 to her former glory as one of the strongest RF self-buffers in the game. Even if you do not have her limited SPEQ, the GZ SPEQ is still worth grabbing for the strong stat buffs.
