ESP32-S3 Monitor

This PCB design combines ESP32-S3, MPU6050, and MAX30102 for real-time motion and heart rate monitoring. Powered by the ESP32-S3 platform, it offers a compact solution for biometric and health applications.

Pulsating Current

This circuit ensures a steady power supply from a power bank, even under low current loads from a microcontroller. Using the NE555 IC, it stabilizes fluctuating or pulsating current, preventing the power bank from turning off unexpectedly.

RNN LSTM Model

This image displays the architecture of an RNN LSTM model designed for Human Activity Recognition (HAR). The model consists of two LSTM layers to capture sequential patterns in the data, complemented by dropout layers to prevent overfitting. Dense layers are used to produce a 4-class classification output. The model has a total of 461,124 trainable parameters.
